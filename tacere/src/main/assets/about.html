Tacere automatically silences your phone whenever there is an active event in the calendar.

  • Adjust your preferences for each event, or let Tacere silence everything by default.
  • Tap an event to cycle the ringer state.
  • Long press an event to clear its settings.
  • If you only need to silence during specific events set your ringer type to normal in the settings and select only the events of interest from the list.
